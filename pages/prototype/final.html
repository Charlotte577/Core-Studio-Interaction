Ideation

Based on the research, I find out that most of the people think the dashboard is not clear enough to use. People think that the layout of the dashboard maybe confused sometimes if they have too many classes. I also find out that not many people will actully open the calendar to check. Therefore, my idea is to combine the calendar, the timeline with the courses together. I also do another version to simpify the layout design, and make it more easy to use.
